RESEARCH PHASE


Online shopping can often prove challenging due to the inherent disconnect between consumers' preferences and the platforms they use. Although e-commerce has transformed the retail industry, it usually fails to recognize and accommodate unique user preferences. Many online shopping platforms are unable to correctly identify a user's specific preferences, wants, and styles, which results in a flood of useless product recommendations and a laborious search experience.



In order to create a seamless user experience for online shopping customers, we need to address these painpoints:



Tedious Search Experience


The traditional search and filter functions on e-commerce platforms often require users to spend a significant amount of time browsing through numerous products to find what they're looking for.

Generic Product Recommendations


Many online shopping platforms offer generic product recommendations that lack personalization to the user, leading to frustration and dissatisfaction among users.

Overwhelming Choices


The abundance of choices on e-commerce platforms can overwhelm users, making it difficult for them to make informed purchasing decisions that are suitable for them.
